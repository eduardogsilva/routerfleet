Welcome to RouterFleet

RouterFleet is an open-source platform designed to simplify and centralize router configuration backups, helping you keep your devices protected with reliable and automated backup routines.

Currently supported platforms:

  • MikroTik (RouterOS)
  • OpenWrt
  • Ubiquiti (airOS)

Support for additional devices is continuously evolving. If your hardware is not yet supported, you can request compatibility by following the submission guidelines described in the discussion.

Project Repository Request Device Support

RouterFleet is actively developed and community-driven. Feedback, suggestions, and contributions are always welcome.
